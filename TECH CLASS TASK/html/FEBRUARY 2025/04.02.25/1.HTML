Ancient PracticeYoga is over 5,000 years old, originating in ancient India as a spiritual, physical, and mental discipline.
Meaning of Yoga: The word "yoga" comes from the Sanskrit word "yuj," meaning "to unite" or "to join." It refers to the union of body, mind, and spirit.
Types of Yoga: There are many types of yoga, including Hatha, Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Kundalini, and Bikram. Each focuses on different aspects, such as movement, breathwork, or meditation.
